About AfriyieCare

At AfriyieCare, we believe that everyone deserves to age gracefully, recover safely, and live with dignity at the place they call home. Our name, inspired by the values of compassion and respect, reflects our commitment to providing reliable, culturally sensitive care that truly makes a difference.

Our Vision

To be a trusted partner in compassionate home care — empowering individuals to live with dignity, comfort, and independence in the place they call home.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver exceptional, person-centered care that supports the physical, emotional, and social well-being of every client. We strive to provide safe, reliable, and compassionate home care through empathy, integrity, and professional excellence — giving families peace of mind and clients the freedom to thrive at home.

Our Services

We offer a full range of personalized home care services designed to promote comfort, safety, and peace of mind, whether for short-term recovery or long-term support.

Elderly Care

Compassionate care that supports seniors with comfort, dignity, and personalized attention.

Daily Living Assistance

Help with bathing, dressing, mobility, toileting, and meal preparation to maintain independence.

Medication Management

Timely reminders and monitoring to ensure medications are taken safely and consistently.

Dementia & Memory Care

Gentle, structured care for individuals with Alzheimer’s or other memory challenges.

End-of-Life & Hospice Care

Compassionate comfort care for clients and families navigating life’s final stages.

Respite Care

Temporary care services allowing family caregivers to rest and recharge with peace of mind.

Babysitting & Child Care

Reliable and nurturing child care that ensures your little ones are safe, engaged, and cared for.

General Cleaning

Complete home cleaning services designed to create a comfortable, hygienic, and stress-free environment.
